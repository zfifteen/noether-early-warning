# noether-early-warning v0.1.0

This release freezes the validated core result of the repository.

It includes:

- the validated four-benchmark claim package `B1` through `B4`
- the consolidated benchmark suite artifacts supporting that package
- the technical note PDF at [`../technical-note/export/noether-early-warning-technical-note.pdf`](../technical-note/export/noether-early-warning-technical-note.pdf)
- the detector-latency sweep as supporting robustness evidence rather than a fifth benchmark

Core validated results:

- `B1`: drift becomes detectable before direct symmetry detection in the gradual regime
- `B2`: the ordering reverses in the instant-break control
- `B3`: under a fixed 300-step budget, drift is the more sensitive detector
- `B4`: at the exact drift alarm time, direct symmetry is still below threshold in the supportive majority

Supporting robustness result:

- the latency-normalized detector sweep preserves the median excess-lead sign split across `45/45` tested settings
